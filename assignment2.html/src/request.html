I successfully got a job at Google as a web developer after many years of training and experience.

My work included developing amazing web applications, collaborating with talented colleagues, and solving complex technical problems. While working at Google, I was constantly learning and developing, introducing the latest technologies and being inspired by innovations.

After a successful experience at Google, my passion for innovation and software development caught the attention of Apple. I persevered, went through several interviews and became part of the team, introducing my skills into the world of Apple products.

At Apple, I was engaged in web development, creating modern and functional interfaces for web applications. My work was at the intersection of design and technical part, which allowed me to implement innovative ideas in products that meet Apple's high quality standards.

At 45, after a year off, I decided to restart my career. With perseverance and self-education training, I mastered web development skills and created an impressive portfolio. Then I submitted an application to Instagram and, thanks to my experience and passion, became part of their Czech web developer team.

At Instagram, I worked as a web developer, creating and improving the user interface and functionality. My expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and UX design has helped improve the user experience of millions of users, and teamwork has contributed to the growth of my skills and career development.
